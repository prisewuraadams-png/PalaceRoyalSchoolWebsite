How to Apply

Admission Process

We have made the admission process simple and parent-friendly. Follow the steps below to begin your child’s enrollment.

1

Fill Application Form

Download and complete the appropriate admission form for your child.

2

Submit Documents

Submit the completed form together with all required supporting documents.

3

Attend Interview

The learner and parent may be invited for an interview or assessment.

4

Receive Offer

Successful applicants receive an admission offer and next-step guidance.
